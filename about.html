About Dr Rahul (PT)

Cardiopulmonary & Orthopedic Physiotherapist

Chief Physiotherapist – JPCRI Jaipur | Lecturer | Rehabilitation Specialist

Professional Profile

Dr Rahul (PT) is a physiotherapist based in Jaipur specializing in cardiopulmonary and orthopedic rehabilitation. He serves as Chief Physiotherapist at JPCRI and is actively involved in clinical rehabilitation, physiotherapy education and academic training.

Clinical Specialization

Cardiac Rehabilitation
Pulmonary Rehabilitation
Orthopedic Physiotherapy
Post-Surgical Rehabilitation
Exercise Therapy & Biomechanics
Chronic Disease Rehabilitation

Academic & Teaching Work

Dr Rahul delivers lectures and academic sessions for physiotherapy students and clinicians in areas including rehabilitation science, exercise therapy, biomechanics and cardiopulmonary physiotherapy. His teaching focuses on clinical reasoning and evidence-based rehabilitation.

Professional Roles

Chief Physiotherapist – JPCRI Jaipur
Physiotherapy Lecturer
Clinical Rehabilitation Specialist
CME & Workshop Faculty

Rehabilitation Philosophy

Rehabilitation should restore function, improve quality of life and support long-term health. Dr Rahul’s approach integrates exercise therapy, patient education and progressive functional rehabilitation tailored to each individual.
