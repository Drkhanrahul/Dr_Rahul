About Dr Rahul Khan

Cardiopulmonary & Orthopedic Physiotherapist, Lecturer, and Rehabilitation Specialist based in Jaipur.

Professional Profile

Dr Rahul Khan (PT) is a physiotherapist and rehabilitation specialist with clinical and academic experience in cardiopulmonary and orthopedic physiotherapy. He serves as Chief Physiotherapist at JPCRI Jaipur, where he leads rehabilitation programs for cardiac, pulmonary, and musculoskeletal conditions.

Alongside clinical practice, Dr Rahul is actively involved in physiotherapy education as a lecturer and faculty for academic sessions, workshops, and continuing medical education (CME) programs. His work focuses on exercise therapy, biomechanics, and evidence-based rehabilitation.

Clinical Expertise

  • Cardiac Rehabilitation
  • Pulmonary Rehabilitation
  • Orthopedic Physiotherapy
  • Post-Surgical Rehabilitation
  • Exercise Therapy & Biomechanics
  • Chronic Disease Rehabilitation

Academic & Teaching Work

Dr Rahul delivers lectures and training sessions for physiotherapy students and clinicians on rehabilitation science, exercise therapy, and biomechanics. He regularly conducts academic teaching, workshops, and CME sessions aimed at improving clinical physiotherapy practice.

Rehabilitation Approach

Dr Rahul’s rehabilitation approach integrates clinical assessment, therapeutic exercise, and functional recovery principles. Treatment programs are designed to restore mobility, improve physiological function, and support long-term health outcomes in cardiac, pulmonary, and musculoskeletal conditions.
